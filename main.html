MEET YOUR DOCTOR OF PHYSICAL THERAPY!


Dr. Visalam (Vis) Vasanth obtained her Doctor of Physical Therapy degree from Rutgers University (formerly UMDNJ). She has practiced as a pediatric physical therapist in Beverly Hills, CA and all over New Jersey. She has experience in school based pediatrics, outpatient pediatrics, and early intervention. In her spare time, she loves building websites and has a passion for the use of technology in pediatric physical therapy.
